Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around branches.

Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Teddy Bear

  • Teddy bears are soft plush toys that are shaped like baby bears.
  • Real bears, on the other hand, are ferocious creaturs that have strong claws.
  • Teddy bears are very good for cuddling kids to sleep.

Panda

  • Giant pandas have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Giant pandas' eyes have vertical slits for pupils instead of normal bears' eyes.
  • Giant pandas learn to climb at 5 months old.

Cicada

  • As babies, cicades can survive a massive fall.
  • Only male cicadas can make a loud whirring sound.
